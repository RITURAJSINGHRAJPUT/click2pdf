Return Policy

Last updated:

Welcome to Click2PDF. Because our services are entirely digital and involve document processing credits that are consumed upon usage, we have specific terms regarding returns.

1. Non-Returnable Items

Digital credits, subscription plans, and processed documents are generally non-returnable once they have been consumed or generated by the user. If a document has been successfully processed using our tools, the associated credit is considered utilized.

2. Service Interruptions

If you experience a technical failure on our end that consumes your credits without delivering the promised service, you may be eligible for a return of those specific credits to your account balance. Please contact our support team with your transaction details.

3. Contact Us

For more information about our privacy practices, if you have questions, or if you would like to make a complaint, please contact us by e-mail at support@click2pdf.in or through our Contact Page.
